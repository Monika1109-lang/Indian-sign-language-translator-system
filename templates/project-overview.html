ISL Translator Overview

Features

The ISL Translator is designed to bridge the communication gap between the deaf and hard-of-hearing community and the hearing world. Here are some key features:

  • Real-time Translation: Translate Indian Sign Language (ISL) gestures into text and speech in real-time.
  • Multi-Language Support: Provides text and speech output in multiple Indian languages.
  • Offline Functionality: Works without an internet connection for seamless communication.
  • Customizable Interface: User-friendly interface with customizable options to suit individual needs.
  • Extensive ISL Library: Includes a comprehensive library of ISL signs and gestures.

Instructions

To use the ISL Translator, follow these simple steps:

  • Step 1: Launch the application on your device.
  • Step 2: Select the desired output language from the dropdown below.
  • Step 3: Begin signing in front of the camera. The application will automatically recognize and translate your signs into text and speech.
  • Step 4: Use the translated text and speech output to communicate with the hearing world.
  • Step 5: Customize the settings to improve accuracy and performance as needed.
